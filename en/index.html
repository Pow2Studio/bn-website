Our team of legal professionals has extensive experience in investigating, procuring, and enforcing intellectual property rights, as well as in licensing and other business transactions in which intellectual property and technology are key components. We offer our clients comprehensive and competent assistance with all aspects of intellectual property law and related fields. Areas of expertise include:

We offer strategic advice to our clients for of obtaining, protecting, and enforcing their intellectual property rights on a national, European or international level. We also offer advice in conflict situations with other holders of property rights.

Our Philosophy

Beckord & Niedlich is a client-driven, full service law firm, covering all fields and aspects of intellectual property law. It is our goal to successfully represent our clients’ interests, achieving the best possible protection of their innovations – and doing so in a time- and cost-effective manner.
