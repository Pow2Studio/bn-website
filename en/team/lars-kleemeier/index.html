Lars Kleemeier

M.Sc.

Lars Kleemeier studied physics at the LMU in Munich. He completed his master programme in 2014 with an additional qualification in the field of biophysics. For his master thesis, he developed a microfluidic device for the investigation of mechanically sensitive polymeric proteins with confocal microscopy.

Subsequently, he worked for a digital network solutions provider in the professional audio industry, where he was responsible for device testing and automation test procedures related to analogue and digital audio signal conversion and transmission. He was also responsible for supervising OEM production setup, training of technical staff and communication between the collaborating managements at a partner company in the USA.

Lars Kleemeier joined our law firm in 2015 as a patent professional.
