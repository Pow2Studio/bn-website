Partner in Cooperation: Dr. Klaus Latzel graduated from Munich’s Technical University with a Master’s degree in chemistry. He received his Ph.D. in the field of organometallic chemistry, investigating new preparation methods of complex compounds and their analytics.

Subsequently, up until his training as a patent attorney, he worked for a large industrial enterprise in the field of catalysis for the production of PVC products.

Since 1999, Klaus has been working in the field of intellectual property law. During his training for the qualification as Patent Attorney, Klaus worked with renowned Munich patent law firms catering to the needs of a large international client base. He also provided IP advice in the field of biotechnology.

He is also a tutor in European Intellectual Property Law at the International Department of CEIPI at the University of Strasbourg.
